Step 2 — Use It

Go to any Walt Disney World page while logged in, then click the bookmarklet.

1
Show bookmarks bar

Chrome: ⌘+Shift+B · Safari: View → Show Favorites Bar

2
Drag the gold button above to the bar

It saves automatically as a bookmark.

3
Go to disneyworld.disney.go.com and log in

Any Disney World page works.

4
Click ✦ Pixie Dust Pro in your bookmarks bar

The overlay appears instantly — no internet needed beyond the Disney page.

1

3
Edit the bookmark URL

4
Use it at Disney

Go to disneyworld.disney.go.com , tap the address bar, type Pixie , tap the bookmark.
