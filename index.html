Setup Instructions

1
Show your bookmarks bar

In Chrome: Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows) or ⌘+Shift+B (Mac). In Safari: View → Show Favorites Bar.

2
Drag the gold button to the bar

Click and drag the "✦ Pixie Dust Pro" button above into your bookmarks bar. It will save as a bookmark automatically.

3
Go to Disney's Lightning Lane page

Navigate to disneyworld.disney.go.com/vas/ and sign in to your Disney account.

4
Click the bookmarklet

Click "✦ Pixie Dust Pro" in your bookmarks bar. The overlay will appear over Disney's page with your live LL data.

1
Copy the bookmarklet code

Tap the button below to copy the code to your clipboard.

Loading…
2
Bookmark any page in Safari

Tap the Share button (box with arrow), then "Add Bookmark". Save it as Pixie Dust Pro.

3
Edit the bookmark's URL

Open your Bookmarks, find "Pixie Dust Pro", and tap Edit. Delete the URL field entirely, then paste the code you copied.

4
Use it at Disney

Go to disneyworld.disney.go.com/vas/ and sign in. Tap the address bar, type Pixie Dust, and tap the bookmark. Done!
